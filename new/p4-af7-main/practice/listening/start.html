Practice Listening

Instructions

This exercise consists of an audio recording followed by a certain number of multiple choice questions, with only one correct answer, about the contents explained in the audio.

The audio can be played a maximum of two times.
Every correct answer is +1 point, and every incorrect answer is -0.33 points. If a question is not answered, it does not deduct points.
The audio transcript, solutions and final score will be available when the exercise is finished.
This exercise takes 15 to 20 minutes to complete.

Recommendations

Read and understand all questions before playing the audio recording.
While the audio is playing, focus on listening and understanding it. Answer the questions after the recording finished playing.
Most of the questions are answered in the audio in the same order that they appear.
